A note from our CEO

Hello to all the great SteppIR owners out there!

We are now moving into antenna season, and I’m having a great time chatting with a lot of you via phone, email and of course at our favorite venues, the ham shows!

We are getting ready to unveil an all-new program called the SteppIR Insider Club. We will be announcing it at Hamvention, but I thought I would share a few details beforehand for our existing customers.

This will be an annual membership opportunity, and with it, you will receive a ton of perks that are not otherwise available:

  • Seasonal discounts and special offers, only for Insider Club Members
  • 40% off discount repair items and extended warranties
  • Access to Insider Club hospitality rooms at major ham radio shows that SteppIR attends
  • Advance notice on new product releases
  • Choice of a SteppIR shirt or hat with all renewals
  • SteppIR “cash” for customer referrals, which can be used for purchasing SteppIR promotional items
  • Ambassador Opportunities at trade shows – get paid to represent SteppIR

We think this program is going to be a lot of fun and will have great value. I can’t talk much more about it right now, but keep an eye out for the May QST ad and more information from our website in the coming months.

Thanks to everyone for their continued support and I will look forward to hearing you on the air!


- John Mertel WA7IR
